### Why This Book

> *"Since before I turned 40, I've been thinking about what kind of product or project I could work on to leave for my kids as some sort of legacy — something nice they can remember. With this website project, I started thinking: can I use it as a practical guide for individuals to learn DevOps principles and have a better understanding of collaboration in today's world, where you're working less with humans and more with AI?"*

This book is the result of that question. It's a combination of two decades of professional experience, academic study, teaching practice, and personal passion — distilled into sixteen actionable plays that anyone can follow.

Juan's goal is not to write a theoretical treatise on DevOps. It's to show — through real code, real pipelines, and real data — that one person with the right principles and the right tools can build production-grade infrastructure. And that in 2026, "the right tools" includes an AI partner.
